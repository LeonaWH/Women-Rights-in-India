Violence at Home

Domestic violence is any abusive behavior in a household that causes physical, psychological, or emotional harm to women.

Sexual Harassment

Sexual harassment is any unwelcome sexual advance or behavior that creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment for women.

Marriage and Divorce

Marriage is a legal contract between two people and divorce is the legal dissolution of that contract by a court.

Child Related

Child-related laws include provisions for abortion rights, maternity leave, and child protection, ensuring healthcare, safety, and equal opportunities for children.

Property Rights

Property rights for women ensure equal ownership, inheritance, and disposal of property as men, without discrimination or preference.

Cyber Bullying

Cyber bullying is the use of technology to harass, intimidate, or threaten women online, causing emotional distress and psychological harm.
